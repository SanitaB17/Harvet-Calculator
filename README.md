## Features

- Calculates **total price** in **â‚¬ and THB**.

## How It Works

1. **Product List**: Each product has a name, weight, and price. The weight is stored in **lb**.
2. **User Input**: Enter quantities (whole numbers) for each product.
3. **Calculation**:
   - All product weights are summed in **lb**.
   - The total weight in **lb** is converted to **kg**.
   - Prices are summed in **Euros (â‚¬)**, then converted to **THB** using a configurable exchange rate.
4. **Results Display**: Shows total weight in **kg / lb** and total price in **â‚¬ / THB**.

## Usage

1. Open the live demo: [Mushroom Harvest Calculator](https://sanitab17.github.io/Harvet-Calculator/)
2. Enter the quantities for your mushroom products.
3. Click **Calculate** to see the total weight and price.
4. Click **Clear** to reset all inputs.
