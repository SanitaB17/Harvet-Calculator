# Mushroom Harvest Calculator

A simple web-based calculator to quickly estimate the total weight and price of mushroom products. The calculator supports multiple products with weights in pounds (lb) and kilograms (kg), and converts totals between units. Prices are displayed in **Euros (€)** and **Thai Baht (THB)**.

---

## Features

- Input quantities for multiple mushroom products.
- Calculates **total weight** in **kg and lb**.
- Calculates **total price** in **€ and THB**.
- Clear button to reset all inputs and totals.
- Only whole numbers allowed for quantities.
- Supports products stored in pounds (lb) or kilograms (kg).
- Realistic conversion for weight and currency.

---

## How It Works

1. **Product List**: Each product has a name, weight, and price. The weight is stored in **lb or kg**.
2. **User Input**: Enter quantities (whole numbers) for each product.
3. **Calculation**:
   - All product weights are summed in **lb**.
   - The total weight in **lb** is converted to **kg** at the end.
   - Prices are summed in **Euros (€)**, then converted to **THB** using a configurable exchange rate.
4. **Results Display**: Shows total weight in **kg / lb** and total price in **€ / THB**.
